Workout Club

A motivational fitness website for Minerva Analytics.

  • Next

Introduction

I was brought onto the project at the early stages when the site was just a community page. Following my arrival, I added the dashboard feature which enables users to set goals for themselves, hold themselves accountable with a schedule and logging feature, and view their progress. In addition, I rebranded the entire site, creating a more fitness-centric logo and reskinning the site following this current branding and color palette.

It was exciting working closely with the analyst and software engineer on the team for this project, constantly putting our heads together to come up with solutions, and iterating on existing features based on user analysis and usage of the site.

Login Modal

Community Page

Here the user can post something about their workout and receive support from their community while motivating others. It's been really fun seeing the community we've grown and the positive energy and support throughout the page.

Dashboard

The dashboard provides one centralized place for users to record their goals, set their schedule, log their workouts, and track their progress throughout their fitness journey.

Schedule

Guiding the user through the process, once a user creates a goal they now set a schedule to be reminded to work out. The limitations of the schedule settings is our current inability to send reminders at specific set times for each user, so the current design is set to send at different times of day with hopes to improve this in the future.

Log

Here a user can document details of their workout.

Progress

The progress page compiles the data from the user's logs, letting them know their trends of mood during workouts, what workouts made them feel at their best or not so much, and these trends over time. We are hoping to add more features to logging which in turn would enable more progress data displayed.
