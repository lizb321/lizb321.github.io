Introduction

Contrast, currently known as Uplift Financial, requested a resdesign of their landing page for their Smart Savings product. They were losing the majority of users on their landing page, so the main goal in this redesign was to maximize click-through. I approached this by giving the user multiple places to continue to the product, adding transparency about the product, and creating a clean and elegant design that gained the user's trust. This was designed using the program Sketch.
