Top Truisms

A quotes website for Minerva Analytics.

  • Next

Introduction

The purpose of this site is to display quotes to users while enabling a browsing experience, generating advertisement revenue, and getting users to sign up for emails. You can visit the live site here.

In addition to design this project allowed me to dip my toes into React.js for the first time. While there was not enough time on the project for me to code the entire site myself, I got to code the navigation bar, which helped me further understand all of the connecting pieces of a website.

Wireframes

These wireframes were designed using Balsamiq. A featured quote is prominently displayed with reaction options, category suggestions and header navigation, in addition to room for advertisements.

Style Guide

Complementing with the concept of quotes, I wanted the website to have an intellectual and calming feel, choosing typography and colors to best match this.

Quotes Page

Most competitor sites were overwhelming and outdated, so I wanted to differentiate Top Truisms by looking modern, sleek and fun while still maintaining the intellectualism I strived for in the style guide. This was achieved by breaking the categories into 10 total buckets for a cleaner experience, and adding the reactions to a quote for a fun way to interact with the site and differentiate us from the competitors feature-wise. Ads were taken into account and strategically placed throughout the page where they would be seen and generate the highest revenue without taking over the experience.
